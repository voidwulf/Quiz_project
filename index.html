Welcome to History Challenge Quiz!

This quiz will challenge your knowledge of fun facts in history.

To start the quiz, click on the start button. The quiz will ask you a question and offer you four answers. Only one answer is correct. Click on the answer to submit it. After submission, you will be informed if the answer is correct or false and offered the next question. After ten questions, you will be presented with your total score.
